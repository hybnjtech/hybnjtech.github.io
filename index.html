About 个人简介

Yanbo Hu was born in 1986 in Runan, Henan Province. He is a member of the Communist Party of China and currently serves as Associate Professor and Head of the Department of Geological Engineering at the School of Transportation Engineering, Nanjing Tech University. His industrial experience includes positions such as technician, chief engineer, production vice general manager, safety vice general manager and acting general manager in mining construction projects at Xuzhou Mining Group. From 2021 to 2024 he served as Secretary of the First CPC Teaching Faculty Branch and Deputy Director of the Discipline Development Office of the School, and from 2022 to 2023 he was Vice General Manager of Shandong Energy Group Luxi Mining Technology Co., Ltd.

胡彦博，汉族，河南汝南人，中共党员，1986 年出生。现任南京工业大学交通运输工程学院地质工程系主任，教学科研型专任教师，副教授、硕士生导师。曾在徐州矿务集团矿建项目担任技术员、技术主管、总工程师、生产副总经理、安全副总经理、副总经理（主持工作）；2021–2024 年任南京工业大学交通运输工程学院教师第一党支部书记、学院学科建设办副主任；2022–2023 年兼任山东能源集团鲁西矿业科技副总。

Background: Mining Engineering (B.Eng.) & Geological Resources and Geological Engineering (Ph.D.), China University of Mining and Technology; Postdoctoral research jointly with China Energy.

教育背景：采矿工程学士、地质资源与地质工程博士（中国矿业大学）；中国矿业大学（北京）与国家能源集团联合培养博士后。
